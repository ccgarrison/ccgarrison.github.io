ROA

Roanoke is considered the Star City and it sits in the Southeast corner of Virginia.

It is a small town nestled beautifully by the Blue Ridge Mountains.

People who visit enjoy:

  1. Viewing the city from the Mill Mountain Star
  2. Hiking the many trails
  3. Walking the the downtown City Market with weekend vendors, local shops and restaurants.

However you choose to get around in the area:

  • Drive
  • Bike
  • Hike

It's going to be a picturesque adventure!
